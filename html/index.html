The Reason I wanted to go to Epicodus

I always had a suspicion I would study to be a developer. My days at Akron had taught me that. Attending a few meetups and speaking with those who had knowledge of such things peaked my interest. In class we commonly used stats programs to process huge amounts of information that would spit out a nice graph telling a story about what all the otherwise was "just" numbers. I remember thinking how cool it would be to save time like that in other places in life.
